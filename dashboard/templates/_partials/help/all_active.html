All Active Work

What is this page?

All active work aggregates every running, pending, and in-flight item across all registered projects — a cross-project summary of the entire platform.

What can I do here?

  • Get a bird's-eye view of all active work across all projects
  • Spot bottlenecks or queue build-ups at a glance
  • Drill into any item or batch from the unified table

Vocabulary

Work item
Feature, Incident, or CR — tracked in the DB, scheduled by the daemon.
Batch
The daemon's scheduling unit — groups items for a single run.
Daemon
The background process that polls and drives all execution.
