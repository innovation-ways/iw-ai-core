Quality KPIs

What is this page?

Weekly regression-rate quality metrics alongside throughput. Each Incident can be classified as regression (introduced by a prior merge), pre-existing (known before any recent merge), or unknown (not yet triaged).

What can I do here?

  • View the regression rate (regressions ÷ merges) for the current week
  • Inspect the 12-week trend chart to spot improving or worsening patterns
  • Classify Incidents on their detail page to feed the KPI numerator
  • Accept heuristic suggestions via iw regression-classify --incident I-NNNNN --accept 1

Vocabulary

Merges/week
Count of work items with status done merged in the week.
Regressions/week
Count of Incidents classified as regression in the week.
Regression rate
regressions ÷ merges for the week. Expressed as a percentage. Zero when no merges occurred.
