Search

What is this page?

Search runs full-text search across all work items, documents, and research in this project — powered by PostgreSQL FTS with ranking and typo tolerance.

What can I do here?

  • Type any keyword to find matching work items, doc titles, or research headings
  • Results are ranked by relevance and update as you type
  • Click any result to navigate directly to it

Vocabulary

Work item
Feature, Incident, or CR — indexed by ID, title, and body text.
Fragment
A section of a document that is indexed and returned as a search hit.
