Research Library

What is this page?

Research collects filed research documents for this project — curated investigations, technology evaluations, and market analyses produced via the /iw-research workflow.

What can I do here?

  • Browse all filed research documents
  • Open any research doc to read the full findings
  • File a new research request via the /iw-research command

Vocabulary

Work item
A Feature, Incident, or CR — research docs may be attached to any of these.
Daemon
The background orchestration process that polls the DB and drives agent execution.
