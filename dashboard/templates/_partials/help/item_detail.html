Item Detail

What is this page?

Item detail is the deep-dive view for a single work item — its design doc, all generated reports, fix cycle history, evidence log, and execution artefacts.

What can I do here?

  • Read the approved design document for this item
  • Browse generated reports, artefacts, and logs
  • Follow fix cycles from first failure through to resolution
  • View evidences attached to this item

Vocabulary

Work item
Feature, Incident, or CR — the atomic unit of work.
Fix cycle
An auto-triggered re-run after a step failure.
Evidence
A structured artefact (screenshot, log excerpt, doc excerpt) attached to an item to justify a decision or capture a result.
