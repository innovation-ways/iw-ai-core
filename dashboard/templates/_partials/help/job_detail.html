Job Detail

What is this page?

Job detail shows the full runtime log and status progression of a single background job — index build, doc render, or research draft generation.

What can I do here?

  • Stream the live log as the job runs
  • Cancel a stuck or misfired job
  • Inspect errors and retry if possible
