Keep-Alive

What is this page?

Keep-alive monitors the daemon's last-seen heartbeat and alerts if the polling loop has stalled — a guard against silent daemon crashes or network partitions.

What can I do here?

  • Check the daemon's last heartbeat timestamp
  • Configure alert thresholds
  • Trigger a manual daemon restart if the process appears stuck

Vocabulary

Daemon
The background process that polls every 60 s and drives all agent execution.
Job
Any background operation tracked by the platform.
