Queue & Backlog

What is this page?

The queue shows all approved (ready-to-run) work items at the top and draft items awaiting review below.

What can I do here?

  • Select one or more approved items and click Create Batch to group them for execution
  • Approve a draft item to move it into the ready queue
  • Cancel any item that is no longer needed

Vocabulary

Work item
A Feature, Incident, or Change Request — the unit of work scheduled by the daemon.
Batch
A named group of work items launched together in a single daemon execution pass.
Approval
The act of moving a draft item to approved status so the daemon can schedule it.
