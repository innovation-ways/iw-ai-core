## Severity Guide

- CRITICAL: missing FK, missing PK column, `make migration-check` red, schema drift.
- HIGH: missing NOT NULL on `ignored_by` or `ignored_at`, missing `server_default` (would force NULL inserts).
- MEDIUM: comment-only nits.
- LOW: import-order, naming.
