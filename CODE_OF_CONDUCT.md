# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v3](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/3/0/code_of_conduct/) as its community standard.

The full text of the Code of Conduct is available at the canonical source: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/3/0/code_of_conduct/

By participating in this project's spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, chat channels, events), you agree to abide by the terms of that Code of Conduct.

## Enforcement Contact

Reports of conduct concerns should be sent to **info@innovation-ways.com**.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

## Response

We acknowledge receipt of reports within **3 business days** and aim to reach resolution within **14 days** for most cases. Resolutions follow the enforcement ladder defined in the Contributor Covenant:

1. Correction (private feedback)
2. Warning
3. Temporary ban
4. Permanent ban
